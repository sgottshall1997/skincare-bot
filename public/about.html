About GlowBot

GlowBot is an AI-powered skincare content generator designed for affiliate marketers, influencers, and creators. It transforms trending skincare products into ready-to-publish content for platforms like TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube Shorts.

How It Works

  • Prompt-Based AI Generation: Uses OpenAI to generate influencer captions, demo scripts, product comparisons, and more, based on selected tone and template.
  • Affiliate Integration: You can include your Amazon link to generate monetized content quickly.
  • Automation-Ready: Can integrate with Make.com or Zapier to auto-post across channels.

Inspiration

This tool follows the framework looks to automate product selection, content generation, and social reach for affiliate success in under 10 minutes.
